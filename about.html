Itachi Uchiha is a fictional character from a well known anime called Naruto. This anime was created than none other than Masashi Kishimoto. Itachi Uchiha is the older brother of one of the protagonist; Sasuke Uchiha. Itachi was one of the many prodigy's in the Hidden Leaf Village. He later became a villan killing his whole clan to protect the Hidden Leaf Village and his little brother. He later ends up joining the Akatuski; where he ends up being a double spy. This led him to a long painful road, where hate will replace love. His character was meant to make Sasuke stronger so he can kill Itachi so the world can view Sasuke as a hero. However, his plann back fired because Sasuke ended up trying to destroy the Hidden Leaf Village and was known as a criminal. Later he ended up fighting Itachi. Sasuke ends up killing his older brother. As a result, Itachi ends up dying as a hero and being a loyal Leaf Shinobi. Sasuke gains the externam mangekyou sharingan. Itachi gets revived and saves many people from the Fourth Great Shinobi War. He stops Kabuto who revived some of the most powerful shinobi in history. The people who got revived ended up leaving and Itachi helped the Allied Shinobi win the War.
